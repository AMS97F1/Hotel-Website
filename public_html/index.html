Welcome to Radford Hotel

Radford Hotel is the premier holiday accommodation located right in the heart of the historical city of Coventry, West Midlands.

First established in 1946, Radford Hotel has been providing quality accommodation for over 50 years. Situated less than a mile away from Coventry City Centre, our highly trained staff and state-of-the-art facilities will guarantee you have nothing but the best of times with us.

Whether you're here for leisure or business, you can rest assured and simply enjoy your stay.
